# Technical Indicators (ta library)

* [Technical Indicators Introduction](introduction.md) - Introduction to the OpenAlgo ta technical indicator library and how to use it
* [Trend Indicators](trend.md) - Trend indicators including SMA, EMA, WMA, HMA, VWMA, KAMA, ALMA, ZLEMA, DEMA/TEMA, Supertrend and Ichimoku Cloud
* [Momentum Indicators](momentum.md) - Momentum indicators including RSI, MACD, Stochastic, CCI, Williams %R, Balance of Power, Elder Ray, Fisher Transform and Connors RSI
* [Volatility Indicators](volatility.md) - Volatility indicators including ATR, Bollinger Bands, Keltner Channel, Donchian Channel, NATR, RVI, Chandelier Exit, Historical Volatility, Ulcer Index and STARC Bands
* [Volume Indicators](volume.md) - Volume indicators including OBV, VWAP, MFI, ADL, CMF, EMV, Force Index, NVI, PVI, Volume Oscillator, VROC, Klinger Volume Oscillator, PVT and RVOL
* [Statistical Indicators](statistical.md) - Statistical indicators including Linear Regression, Linear Regression Slope, Correlation, Beta, Variance, Time Series Forecast, Rolling Median, Median Bands and Rolling Mode
* [Hybrid Indicators](hybrid.md) - Hybrid indicators including ADX, Aroon, Pivot Points, Parabolic SAR, DMI, Williams Fractals and Random Walk Index
* [Utility Functions](utility.md) - Utility functions for indicator pipelines including crossover, crossunder, cross, highest, lowest, change, roc, stdev, rising, falling, exrem, flip and valuewhen
